### About the course
  
AAE101 is an introductory course that explains how individuals, firms, and governments make decisions under conditions of scarcity. It covers core concepts such as supply and demand, prices, markets, incentives, and efficiency, and uses them to understand everyday economic phenomena like price changes, wages, and taxation. It also includes basic ideas of game theory.
